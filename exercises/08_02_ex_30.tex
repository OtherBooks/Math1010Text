{ A hiker determines the bearing to a lodge from her current position is S$40^{\circ}$W.  She proceeds to hike 2 miles at a bearing of S$20^{\circ}$E at which point she determines the bearing to the lodge is S$75^{\circ}$W.  How far is she from the lodge at this point?  Round your answer to the nearest hundredth of a mile.}
{She is about 3.02 miles from the lodge}
