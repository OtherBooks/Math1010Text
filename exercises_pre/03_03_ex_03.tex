\begin{itemize}
\item  All of the real zeros lie in the interval $[-13,13]$
\item  Possible rational zeros are $\pm 1$, $\pm 2$, $\pm 3$, $\pm 4$, $\pm 6$, $\pm 12$
\item  There are 2 or 0 positive real zeros;  there are 2 or 0 negative real zeros
\end{itemize}
